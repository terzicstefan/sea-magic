ABOUT US

Agency background

"Sea Magic" is a company for transporting passengers at sea, founded in 2023. Our skipper brings absolute safety to your sea adventure with his knowledge and experience. He obtained his license while attending the maritime school in Kotor and graduating from the Faculty of Nautical Tourism.
With years of experience working for other companies, we bring you expertise and professionalism as part of every trip we organize.

Our Mission

Our fleet includes one speedboat, the Marinello Eden 22, which can accommodate a maximum of 10 passengers.

Comfort and pleasure are guaranteed, while our knowledge of the region we are traveling through will allow all travelers to get to know the region better and indulge in the beauty of our open seas!

In our tours, the pick-up location is flexible and can be arranged according to your wishes.

We offer both private and group tours, providing personalized experiences tailored to your needs and desires.

To reach a port we must sail, sometimes with the wind,
and sometimes against it. But we must not drift or lie at anchor.
